1. MacroSheds Meeting: Portal Discussion

This document outlines potential upgrades and patches for the MacroSheds portal, specifically to facilitate a discussion about our group’s priorities and needs for what a stable “beta” may look like. The primary goal of this discussion is to agree on the necessary components of a MacroSheds portal “beta” minimum viable product, and then rank-prioritize any non-essential additions and fixes.

1.1. Layout

  • make watershed info table pivot in fullscreen mode, so each site in the bucket is a row, and its attributes are columns. this table could also be downloadable as-is
  • fine-grain layout edits for:
    • eliminate text running outside of containers
    • eliminate “jumbling” containers
    • eliminate overlapping text or icons
    • eliminate bottom-of-screen discrepencies
    • test and ensure all fixes consistent across browsers and screen sizes

1.2. Maps & Geospatial

  • watershed “zoom” button or favorite tag
  • layers we have now
    • NLCD
    • NLCD change
    • Tree Canopy
    • Tree Canopy Change
    • SMGC Geology
    • Impervious Surfaces
    • EPA EcoRegions
  • layers we want?
    • Weather
    • NHD Flowlines
    • Soils
    • Deposition (which elements?)

1.3. Data & Visualization

  • ’data forge’ tab, auto-ggplot that allows drop-in axes, and copying source code
  • ’best-foot-forward’ feature where a selected site will automatically populate with it’s most robust stream chemistry measurement

1.4. Narrative

  • “data stories,” even reach out to networks for favorite stories in their datasets
  • extended site tour, engaging new features
  • more investment in about and notes tabs, more context and information about the data and team
